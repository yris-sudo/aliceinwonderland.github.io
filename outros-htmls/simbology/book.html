Curiosities About "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland"

First Publication: "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland" was first published in 1865 and quickly became a classic, captivating readers of all ages.

John Tenniel's Illustrations: The book features illustrations by John Tenniel, whose detailed drawings helped shape the visual identity of characters like the Cheshire Cat and the Mad Hatter.

Iconic Characters: Tenniel's unique interpretations of the characters have influenced their portrayals in various adaptations, establishing their visual representations in popular culture.

Symbolism in Illustrations: Tenniel's artwork is rich in symbolism, with details that enhance the narrative and reflect Alice's changing perceptions throughout her adventures.

Cultural Impact: Tenniel's illustrations have become iconic, inspiring countless adaptations and continuing to influence artists and illustrators around the world.
